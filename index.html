EX-CHANGE Events

EX-CHANGE events examine the three challenges the SoA addresses through the Pedagogies 2020 through series that each include a faculty-student research showcase, alumni panel, and portfolio review for students (by RSVP).

  • Launch & Artificial Intelligence Series, Thursday Sept. 09, 4:30-7:30pm (CFA 214 and Great Lawn; Events Livestreamed on Zoom) (Register)
    • 4:30: 4:30: Introduction to the EX-CHANGE exhibition by Omar Khan, Leah Wulfman & Elana Schlenker, followed by a research showcase discussing the school’s contributions to discourse around artificial intelligence, coordinated by Joshua Bard and Daniel Cardoso Llach.
    • 5:45: Alumni panel hosted by Inter • punct
    • 6:30: Portfolio reviews for current students by alumni (RSVP) & catalog pick-up on the CFA Lawn (weather permitting)
  • Social Justice Series, Thursday Sept. 16, 4:30-7:30pm (Register)
    • 4:30: Research showcase discussing the school’s contributions to discourse around social justice, coordinated by Dr. Erica Cochran-Hameen and Kai Gutschow.
    • 5:30: Alumni panel hosted by NOMAS
    • 6:30: Portfolio reviews for current students by alumni (RSVP)
  • Climate Change Series, Thursday Sept. 23, 4:30-7:30pm (Register)
    • 4:30: Research showcase discussing the school’s contributions to discourse around climate change, coordinated by Mary-Lou Arscott.
    • 5:30: Alumni panel hosted by AIAS
    • 6:30-7:30: Portfolio reviews for current students by alumni (RSVP)
